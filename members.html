Guild Leaders

Kabal was originally founded in Guild Wars by the following three individuals. These three continue to lead and administrate the guild with the help of the guild officers.

Guild Officers

Kabal officers are members who took it upon themselves to go above and beyond the call of duty and showed leadership in the greater structure of the guild and community. These individuals volunteered to help lead the guild in an official capacity and were promoted to officer status by the guild leadership.

Paragons

Paragons are those who are recognized as trusted and helpful members of the guild. They have contributed greatly to Kabal and have been in the guild for a decent amount of time.
